About Me

I specialize in the intersection of IT Support, Cybersecurity, and EdTech. My journey started with a fascination for how systems work and how to fix them when they don't.

With a foundation in technical support and a Cisco Ethical Hacking certification, I bring a "security-first" perspective to every project. I don't just focus on whether a system works; I focus on how it can be hardened against vulnerabilities while remaining seamless for the end-user.

Beyond the hardware and code, I am a passionate advocate for EdTech, STEM education and Digital Literacy, focusing on how technology can improve learning access, digital skills development and innovation in education systems.
